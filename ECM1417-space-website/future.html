Upcoming Space Missions

Artemis III – Return to the Moon (2026)

NASA’s Artemis III mission aims to return humans to the lunar surface, including the first woman and first person of colour to walk on the Moon.

Mars Sample Return Mission (2028)

A joint NASA–ESA mission designed to collect and return Martian soil samples to Earth for detailed scientific analysis.

Europa Clipper – Ocean World Exploration (2030)

Europa Clipper will investigate Jupiter’s icy moon Europa, searching for conditions suitable for life beneath its frozen surface.

First Human Mission to Mars (2035+)

NASA and international partners plan to send astronauts to Mars, marking humanity’s most ambitious journey into deep space.
